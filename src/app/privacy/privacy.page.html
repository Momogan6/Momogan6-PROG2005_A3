Privacy & Security Privacy Policy

This Inventory Management App values your privacy and protects all data.

Data Storage

All inventory data is stored locally on your device using localStorage. No data is sent to external servers or third parties.

Data Usage

Data is only used for managing inventory functions. It will never be shared, sold, or used for other purposes.

Security

All data remains on your device and is fully controlled by you. Clearing app storage will delete all data permanently.

Group Project Notice

This application is developed as a group project. We follow strict privacy and security standards.
